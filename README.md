## Understanding ICBC Collision Repair

ICBC collision repairs involve several stages, including damage documentation, estimate preparation, insurer approval, parts ordering, structural repair, refinishing, and final reassembly. The accuracy of early documentation and planning plays a major role in avoiding delays later in the process.

Modern vehicles often require model-specific parts and manufacturer-approved repair procedures, which can affect scheduling and repair duration.
