ICBC Collision Repair Process in Burnaby, BC

An ICBC collision repair involves more than visible body work. The outcome is largely determined by three factors: damage documentation, repair planning, and communication.

Shops also have to sequence work carefully. Structural repairs, refinishing, and reassembly must happen in the correct order to ensure the vehicle is restored safely. Rushing this process can compromise repair quality, which is why reputable ICBC repair shops prioritize accuracy over speed.

One example of an ICBC collision repair shop in Burnaby is Mint Auto Body & Glass ICBC, which emphasizes accurate estimates, proper documentation, and consistent communication.

For drivers, understanding these steps helps set realistic expectations and leads to a smoother ICBC collision repair experience.
